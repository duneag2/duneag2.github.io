--- layout: archive permalink: /personal/ author_profile: true --- Beyond my identity as a researcher, I also embrace my alter ego as a songwriter and author. As a teenager, I dreamed of becoming a journalist and spent over seven years in a student press corps, writing numerous articles. I also wrote lyrics and composed numbers for various musical theater productions.
I think these identities ultimately converge; arranging instrumentals and writing have taught me how to manifest my ideas, a process that mirrors scientific research, where creativity and structure are equally essential. As I move forward, I aspire to live not only as a researcher but fundamentally as a creator, blending scientific rigor with creative expression.
